Moonlit Evenings

Soft radiance, distant friends, and quiet courtyards beneath a silver moon. These poems pair characters with matching pinyin to help you savor every tone.

Guided highlights

Follow moonbeams across river harbors, snow-filled valleys, and lonely inns. The pinyin alongside each line helps you internalize the rhythm of night-time imagery.

  • Homesick reflections and poetic farewells.
  • Scenes set beneath moon and starlight.
  • Ideal for evening study sessions.

Suggested pairings

Listen to guqin instrumentals while reading, and compare tone contours between Li Bai and Liu Zongyuan.
