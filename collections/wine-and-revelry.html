Wine & Revelry

Toasts to friendship, fleeting life, and cups raised under the moon. Practice tones with line-by-line pinyin as you savor each celebratory verse.

Guided highlights

Follow Li Bai’s exuberant voice alongside Tao Yuanming’s rustic calm. Compare diction and tone patterns while keeping pace with each toast.

  • Celebratory poems perfect for gatherings.
  • Side-by-side pinyin for tonal rehearsal.
  • Contextual summaries for each piece.

Hosting idea

Print the characters and pinyin to host a bilingual poetry reading. Pair with jasmine tea or plum wine.
