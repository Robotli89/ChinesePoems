Mountain Retreats

Find quiet companionship in valleys, hermit huts, and snow-fed rivers. Characters, pinyin, and translations keep each landscape within reach.

Guided highlights

Trace the steps of poets who sought stillness away from the capital. Read aloud with the pinyin to feel the cadence of winding paths and tumbling water.

  • Hermit reflections and mist-covered peaks.
  • Perfect for mindfulness and meditation.
  • Pinyin helps follow subtle tonal shifts.

Field note

Bring these poems on a hike and recite them at scenic overlooks to connect breath with verse.
