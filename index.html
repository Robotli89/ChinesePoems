静夜思

李白 · Tang Dynasty

床前明月光， 疑是地上霜。 举头望明月， 低头思故乡。

A quiet moment with Wang Wei

鹿柴 · "Deer Enclosure"

Wang Wei · Tang Dynasty

空山不見人， 但聞人語響。 返景入深林， 復照青苔上。

In empty mountains, no one can be seen, Yet echoing voices ring through the forest. Returning light slips deep among the trees, Again it shines upon the mossy green.

Why this poem matters

Wang Wei's landscape poems harmonize Buddhist tranquility with painterly detail. Each verse invites the reader to slow down, listen closely, and observe the play of light. Our commentary accompanies the original characters with precise translations, so you can feel the silence between the words.

  • Audio recitation: Native pronunciation guide for tone and cadence.
  • Historical notes: Explore the poet's life during the flourishing Tang court.
  • Calligraphy spotlight: Learn the brush techniques echoed in the poem's imagery.
